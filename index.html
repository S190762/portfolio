About Me

Welcome to my Online Portfolio. I am a BSc Computer Games Programming student at The University of Suffolk, Ipswich, UK.

Over the course of my education at UOS I have primarily focused on the programming of computer games using Unity, Unreal Engine as well as my own projects in C++ and Rust.

For my final university project, I created a basic 2D game engine in Rust using modern game development techniques such as Entity Component Systems. In my 2nd Year I worked on a graphics development project using Vulkan.

I am interested in a role in the engine development area of games development, in emerging areas such as Vulkan.
